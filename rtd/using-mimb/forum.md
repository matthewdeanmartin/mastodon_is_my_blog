# Forum

The **Forum** page is an early discussion view for Mastodon is My Blog (MIMB).

Its purpose is to make conversation easier to revisit than it is in a normal feed.

## What it tries to do

Forum looks for multi-person discussions and presents them as conversation entries rather than isolated posts.

Each card tries to show:

- who is involved
- how many replies happened
- when the thread last moved
- a quick preview of the root post

## Filters on this page

Forum currently offers filters like:

- **Most Active**
- **Recent**
- **Friends Only**
- **All Discussions**

This page is still more experimental than People or Content, so treat it as a developing view rather than the final answer to conversation browsing.

## Best use

Forum is most useful when you want to answer:

> "Which conversations are worth opening?"

rather than:

> "What is every reply in order?"

## Current expectation

Use Forum as a discovery surface. When a thread matters, open it and read the full post context from there.
