# Mastodon is My Blog

Mastodon is My Blog (MIMB) is a local-first Mastodon reader and writer that replaces the usual endless mixed feed with a calmer, blog-like experience.

Instead of throwing every post into one river, MIMB helps you read by **person**, **topic**, and **intent**:

- **People** for blog-style reading by account
- **Content** for links, images, software finds, news, and grouped hashtag topics
- **Forum** for discussion-heavy threads
- **Write** for publishing without opening your instance's default interface
- **Admin** for syncing, account setup, catch-up jobs, and content bundles

## What to expect

MIMB is opinionated:

- It is built for **finite reading**, not doomscrolling.
- It expects you to **refresh on purpose** instead of consuming a live firehose.
- It keeps a **local cache** so views can be organized and filtered in ways Mastodon itself does not always offer.
- It can support **more than one Mastodon identity** under one local setup.
