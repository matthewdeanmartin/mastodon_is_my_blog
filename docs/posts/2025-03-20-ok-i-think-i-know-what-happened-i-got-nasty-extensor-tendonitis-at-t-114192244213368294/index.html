@runmattrun March 20, 2025

Ok, I think I know what happened. I got nasty extensor tendonitis at the #marathon. And I have been getting it every time I go #running that is about 10 miles long. The shoe laces are loose. The feet swell up by the end of the race. What is going on?! Well, lots of googling and I think it is... #hyponatremia. It is one of the most common long distance #runner problems and a symptom is hands and feet swelling up. Which in turn makes my shoes too small and I get extensor tendonitis.
