Observations people using AI for writing - Yes, some yahoos think spamming docs...

@mistersql March 13, 2024

Observations people using AI for writing
- Yes, some yahoos think spamming docs no one read will make them rich. It won't
- (some) Pros are using them to write 1st drafts. Why? Because pay rates for writing are really low & for some things there isn't a lot of ways to write it
- Some of the worst things about AI writing are solvable engineering problems.
- Writing about socks to sell socks. The bot is doing fine. This isn't exactly the prestige writing future millenniums will remember us for

Self-replies

March 13, 2024

Similar to programming, the people that get the best results from AI assisted writing are highly skilled writers, because you need to have a sense for what is crap & needs to be redone and what is the text that is already fine. Other than cleaning up catastrophic grammar and spelling, the bot can't help an amateur write good material.
