So my economic anxiety is off the charts. As an American, and no one freaks out...

@mistersql February 17, 2025

So my economic anxiety is off the charts. As an American, and no one freaks out like an American, the news is toxic enough that it is making it hard for me to get life done.

I installed Block Website and I think it has freed up a lot of time.
https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/block-website/

Self-replies

February 17, 2025

It freed up so much time, no I have to decide what to do with all this free time. I've resumed reading paper books. Time spent doing activities with the kids is way up. I've started sitting zen again, albeit for really short sits. I'm trying to cook more.

Turns out lifestyle design is hard now. In the good old days, everything was so inefficient, the world designed your lifestyle: driving everywhere to do errands- return Block Buster, paying utility bills in person, commuting,

February 17, 2025

picking up packages at the post office.
