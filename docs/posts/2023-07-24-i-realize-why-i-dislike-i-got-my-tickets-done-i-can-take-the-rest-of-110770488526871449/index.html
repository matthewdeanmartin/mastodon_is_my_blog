I realize why I dislike "I got my tickets done, I can take the rest of the day/week off"-I mean, aside from the federal time card fraud crime part

- Everything's broken
- Our best code is still somewhat broken
- We fix it until we run out of time
-"I got my tickets done" is an arbitrary quitting time, it means "I worked until I didn't feel like working on it anymore"

There is an element of "definition of done" and did it pass process, but that is a fiction we project over the reality of coding
