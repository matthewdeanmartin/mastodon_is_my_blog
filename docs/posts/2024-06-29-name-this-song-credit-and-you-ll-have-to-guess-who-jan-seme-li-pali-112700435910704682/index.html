Name this song! (Credit #Sonnet and you'll have to guess who)

jan seme li pali e kasi nasa lon poka mi?
kon laso li kama anpa lon ni.
pimeja li kama. kule mute li kama wan.
mun loje li lon sewi.

mi tawa kepeken kon.
mi kama wawa lon tenpo pi tawa ike.
suno en pilin ike en pilin utala ale
li lon insa mi.

jan li wile ala e mi la mi o wile e mi!
