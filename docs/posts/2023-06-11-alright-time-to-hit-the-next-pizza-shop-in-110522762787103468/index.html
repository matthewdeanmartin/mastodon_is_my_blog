This take on a vegan pizza is a veggie pizza without cheese. Tasted fine, but it was lacking in either a bean thing, an olive thing or a vegetable that has lots of oil, e.g. a fried eggplant. Without one of those 3 things, it isn't nutritionally in the same category of a pizza (high calorie meal that has all of the vegan food groups). I enjoyed it despite it being poorly though out because good bread with veggies still tastes good.
