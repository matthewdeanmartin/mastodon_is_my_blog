Instances fill up with people until they are forced to close registrations....

@mistersql November 28, 2022

Instances fill up with people until they are forced to close registrations. Registrations can only re-open when they voluntarily give more money to the admin. (If an admin found themselves with $10k a month in donations, they buy more hardware/support more people. Or pocket it)

Not sure this aligns the costs/benefits/incentives. People put out money when they want something, & who wants more users on their instance?
