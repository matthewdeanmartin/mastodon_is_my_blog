@mistersql December 8, 2023

Things I learned during the pandemic
- post-viral fatigue is a thing & can happen for any common cold/flu-like thing (suffering from it right now!) & ranges from barely noticeable to debilitating. As for me, I just feel lazy in the afternoon
- all the viruses have been attacking & damaging my olfactory bulb my whole life & that is why our sense of smell declines as we age
- everytime you get a cough you risk pneumonia & should do all the things to keep the liquids from just sitting in your lungs
