I've been watching "productivity youtube". Reactions
- There isn't one set of advice/systems that works for everyone. Each person is going to have to find something that works for them.
- The reactionary/anti-"productivity youtube" or "toxic productivity" message doesn't take into account that what is toxic productivity for one person & context is survival for another person & context

December 5, 2023

- Most advice/systems is for the "obsessive/compulsive personality disorder type" & for people who are not suffering from their disorder, but actively enjoy it.
- But people of other personality types also need to get things done, too. They're just going to have different effective advice/systems. (For example, the "spoons" system, for people who got problems just getting the essentials done)
